Gluten Free Red Bean Brownie

Recipe Description

Brownies made from red beans

Ingrediants

  • 2 cups of canned red beand
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 3 tablespoons cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup of honey
  • 1 teaspoon backing powder
  • 1 cup of chopped walnuts

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 F
  2. Blend beans in a blender
  3. Beat eggs in a seperate bowl
  4. Add the melted butter, honey, baking powder and cocoa powder and whisk.
  5. Add the pureed beans and chopped walnuts and mix
  6. Pour the mixture into a greased baking dish
  7. Bake until thoothpick comes out clean
