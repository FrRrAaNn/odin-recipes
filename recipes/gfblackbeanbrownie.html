Gluten Free Black Bean Brownie

Recipe Description

Delicious gluten free black bean brownie you can enjoy at any time of the day!

Ingredients

  • 1.5 cups of black beans
  • 2 tbsp cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup of quick oats
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/3 cup pure maple syrup, honey, or agave
  • 1/4 cup coconut oil
  • 2 tsp pure vanilla extract
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 2/3 cup of chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 F
  2. Combine all ingredients (except the chocolate chips) in a good blender and blend until smooth.
  3. Stir chocolate chips into the goup you just made
  4. Pour goup into a 8x8 pan
  5. Bake for 15 to 18 minutes

If they are still goupy try putting then in the fridge over night.
